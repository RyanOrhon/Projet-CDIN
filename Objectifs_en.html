Objectives :

The DUT of the IUT Objectives :
TC DUT The University Diploma of Technology (DUT) in Marketing Techniques (TC) allows: to train students to occupy versatile, autonomous and progressive sales positions, to accompany students in a real professionalizing university course and to prepare a professional integration at level III or further studies.
Internships in a professional environment. In the 1st year, the student carries out a 3-week discovery internship in a company evaluated by a report and a defense. In the 2nd year, in addition to the daily professional practice, he is in charge of the implementation of a particular mission during 8 weeks. A detailed report and a presentation are made to a jury made up of representatives of the teaching team and the company internship supervisor.
MIT DUT The Multimedia and Internet Professions OTC (MIT) trains specialists in the design and networking of digital communication products. This training allows them to acquire skills in the fields of audiovisual, computer graphics, web-oriented computing and communication.
Internships are a key part of these two years. They allow students to get a clear idea of the professions they plan to turn to, from web development agencies to audiovisual and communication production structures. They last two weeks in the first year to facilitate their observation and help them find their way around, and are longer in the second year. At the end of these two internships, the practical reality of the professional world can no longer be ignored. These internships are supplemented by conferences during which practising professionals come to share their experiences.
MOO DUT

The DUT MANAGEMENT OF ORGANIZATIONS (Bac+2 level) trains students capable of assuming management and responsibility in the field of management of private or public organizations. After a common 1st year, students have the choice between 3 options in the 2nd year: Management and Organizational Management, Human Resources Management, Accounting and Financial Management. The third semester can be spent abroad.
Internships. In the 1st year, this is a 4-week discovery internship in a professional environment. In the 2nd year, in addition to the daily professional practice, the student is in charge of the implementation of a particular mission during 8 weeks. During the course of the internship, the department ensures a follow-up requiring exchanges of information with the company. At the end of the internship, the student must support a report before a jury comprising, for example, the company's internship supervisor, the trainee's tutor teacher and another permanent teacher from the department.
IT DUT The Computer Science DUT trains students capable of participating in the design, realization and implementation of computer systems corresponding to the needs of users and companies. To assume these responsibilities, computer scientists must : be technically competent, know the socio-economic environment in which they will have to practice their profession, have a good general culture and communication skills.
Numerous projects, involving 300 hours of work, are carried out over the four semesters: website design and development, games (tetris, awalé, etc.), management databases, etc. They aim to develop : the practical application of knowledge and know-how (research, proposal of solutions, realization, etc.), learning project management methodology: writing specifications, group work, time and deadline management, written and oral communication (in French and English), the development of relational skills: autonomy, initiative, conflict management.
